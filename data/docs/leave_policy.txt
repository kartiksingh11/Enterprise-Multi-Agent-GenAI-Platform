Leave Policy

All full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid time off (PTO) per calendar
year, credited at a rate of 1.5 days per month. Unused PTO can be carried
over into the next year up to a maximum of 10 days; anything beyond that
is forfeited on December 31st.

Sick leave is separate from PTO. Employees receive 10 paid sick days per
year. Sick leave does not carry over and resets each January 1st. A
doctor's note is required for sick leave longer than 3 consecutive days.

Parental leave is available to all employees regardless of tenure.
Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of paid leave. Secondary caregivers
receive 6 weeks of paid leave. Parental leave must be taken within 12
months of the child's birth or adoption date.

To request PTO, employees must submit a request through the HR portal
at least 5 business days in advance for requests under 3 days, or 2
weeks in advance for requests of 3 days or more. Manager approval is
required for all PTO requests.

Unpaid leave of absence may be granted at management discretion for
periods up to 90 days, subject to HR approval and business needs.
