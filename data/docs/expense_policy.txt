Expense Reimbursement Policy

Employees may submit expense reports for business-related costs incurred
while performing their job duties. All expense reports must be submitted
within 30 days of the expense being incurred. Reports submitted after 30
days require VP-level approval and may be denied.

Travel expenses: Economy class flights are covered for all domestic and
international travel under 6 hours. Business class is approved only for
flights longer than 6 hours, or for employees at Director level and above.

Meals during business travel are reimbursed up to $75 per day for
domestic travel and $100 per day for international travel. Alcohol is
not reimbursable under any circumstances.

Hotel accommodations are capped at $250 per night for domestic travel
and $350 per night for international travel, unless the conference or
event venue requires a higher rate, in which case prior approval from a
manager is required.

Home office equipment: New employees receive a one-time $500 stipend for
home office setup (desk, chair, monitor, etc.). This stipend must be used
within the first 90 days of employment and requires receipts for
reimbursement.

Client entertainment expenses up to $150 per person are reimbursable with
prior manager approval and a brief description of the business purpose.

All reimbursement requests must include itemized receipts. Credit card
statements alone are not sufficient documentation.
